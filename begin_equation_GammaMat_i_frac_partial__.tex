\begin{equation}
  \GammaMat_i = \frac{\partial\al_i}{\partial\x_i}
  = \left[\begin{array}{cc}
    \kappa_i + \gamma_{\mathrm{c},i} & \gamma_{\mathrm{s},i} \\
    \gamma_{\mathrm{s},i} & \kappa_i - \gamma_{\mathrm{c},i}
  \end{array}\right].
\end{equation}
In the case of a void this simplifies to $\GammaMat = \kappa \I$.
